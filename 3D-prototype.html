The Prototype

Design(human)design is a tool developed by the MIT Media Lab that randomizes prompts for designers to inspire creativity. Our prompts included creating something that is paradigm shifting, targets a disorder, and uses a CAD program. Our resolved solution was designing a bracelet for people with OCD to customize and wear functionally and fashionably. As part of the project we designed a bracelet using an online CAD program and had it 3D printed at the Engineering lab, we designed a style guide for Embrace to fit that of a mid-end luxury brand, and designed a prototype of the online customizable portion. I worked on all three smaller projects and specifically used my knowledge in UX Design to design the prototype featured here in Sketch and inVision.
